The Best Movies According to Priyanshu

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Batman Begins

Batman Begins teaches us that even in the face of fear and adversity, one can overcome challenges through determination, training, and embracing one's inner strength.

Creed

Creed emphasizes the importance of resilience, hard work, and believing in oneself to overcome obstacles and achieve success, both inside and outside the boxing ring.

Hachi

Hachi teaches us about loyalty, love, and the enduring bond between humans and animals. The film illustrates the profound impact of unwavering dedication and loyalty over time.
